Entrepreneurial Summit 2024

E-Summit

E-Summit 2024 at MLRITM, hosted by Innovation and Incubation Council, is an annual entrepreneurship festival. The summit features various conclaves, including Youth, Innovator, Startup, and Startup Essentials, along with networking, workshops, competitions, and keynote sessions by industry leaders.

Events

E-Summit 2024 features events like the Keynote Session, Startup Expo, Ideathon ,Hackathons, Networking Lounge, and Panel Discussions, with exciting activities such as the Battle of Bands and Virtual Trading League to inspire and connect participants.

Stalls

The E-Summit 2024 features a vibrant marketplace of stalls showcasing innovative products, services, and startups. Visitors can explore a diverse range of exhibits, from cutting-edge technology to sustainable solutions. These stalls will offer opportunities for networking, learning, and potential collaborations.

IIC

The Innovation and Incubation Council (IIC) fosters entrepreneurship by inspiring creativity, providing cutting-edge resources, and connecting innovators with mentors and investors. IIC empowers participants to explore ideas, experiment, and create impactful solutions that drive meaningful change
